In Colombia mild and well-balanced coffee beans are produced. Most of the coffee is grown in Colombia's coffee zone also known as Colombian coffee triangle with cities like Manizales and smaller locations like the picturesque Salento. You can visit some producers and get a guided tour on their coffee farm with the possibility of tasting coffee at the end of your visit. Some of them offer accomodation for a longer stay.
